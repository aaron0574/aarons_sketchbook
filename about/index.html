If you haven't figured it out yet, my name is Aaron Greene and I like to draw.

I'm an artist and graphic designer based out of northern Indiana with over 9 years of experience working as a professional artist. I enjoy illustrating a variety of subjects in many different styles such as impressionism, surrealism, cartooning and abstract art.

I specialize in black and white illustrations...

Favorite Artists: Salvador Dali, Andy Warhol, Robert Crumb, Bob Ross, Gary Larson, Bill Alexander

Specialties: Graphic Design, Digital Illustration, Logo Design, Surrealism, Landscapes
